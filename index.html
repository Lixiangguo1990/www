ABOUT US

Micro Shipping Cultural Communication Co., Ltd (original: Chamfoo Shipping Gift Corporation Ltd), is a creative enterprise located in Shenzhen which focus on the niche market of shipping & logistics subdivision and dedicated to shipping culture and communication.

Micro-shipping was founded by a group of young people who have been engaged in shipping for many years. With the idea of “Coming From Shipping, Coming To Shipping”, we are not only engaged in the creative shipping peripheral products business, but also try to explore the innovation and practice in new area of shipping culture, shipping history, shipping art exchange and communication. Profession,efficiency, quality, service and brand are our persistent pursuit.
