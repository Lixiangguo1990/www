Global ONE-STOP-SHOP Souvenirs Solution For ONE Line H.Q

2018-11-30

ONE Line (Ocean Network Express Pte. Ltd.，) is an all new joint venture by MOL, NYK & K-Line in 2016, which were known as the 3 most famous Japanese ocean liners in the world.

 

Micro Shipping connected with ONE Line H.Q. Singapore for the 1st co-operation since late Oct. 2017. After 5 months of constant communication and understanding with each other, ONE Line & Micro Shipping finally reached an agreement for a order of 15,000.00pcs of popular items such as 1:87/1:50 alloy truck models, container models and container power banks etc.

Micro Shipping provided a ONE-STOP-SHOP solution to ONE Line that, we would handle the whole project from the very beginning of draft design, sampling, mass production, packing and distribution to 55 countries and cities all over the world. 1 month later after the completion of ONE Line Singapore project, ONE Line China H.Q. placed another urgent project for the 1st China Import Expo, again Micro Shipping executed the urgent project smoothly and received high appreciation from ONE Line China H.Q.

 

The 2 projects were successfully implemented and everything was under Micro Shipping’s control and expectation. The success of the 2 projects both proved that Micro Shipping has the strong and integrated competitive power to produce good quality products while providing a very reliable service and achieve the best customers’ satisfaction as always.
